▌ Block 02 — System Icons

Ten files.
One mark.
Every surface.

From a 16-pixel browser tab to a 1200×630 LinkedIn card — the symbol stays readable, the terra cotta hub stays terra cotta, and the silhouette never breaks. Drop-in for any web project under github.com/sypnose-cloud.

§ 02.A  ·  10 RASTER & VECTOR ASSETS

Every size pixel-hinted, not auto-scaled.

Below 24 px the mark switches to the simplified silhouette (variant 1.8): a terra hub on a cream disc with 6 solid satellites. No spokes, no hex perimeter — those become visual noise at small scale.

§ 02.B  ·  OG CARD · ANATOMY

The card that ships every link.

Used for LinkedIn, X, Slack, Discord and iMessage unfurls. Dark slate ground, terra cotta hub, four live metrics in monospace at the foot — the same brand voice as the CLI.

§ 02.C  ·  PWA MANIFEST

Drop-in manifest.json.

§ 02.D  ·  <head> SNIPPET

Paste once. Ship everywhere.

Drop this block inside <head> on every Sypnose property. The OG card is the only image referenced — meta tags do the rest.
