▌ Block 01 — The Mark

One hub.
Six workers.
One command.

The Sypnose mark is a literal diagram of the platform: a single architect node at the center, dispatching parallel agents around a hex perimeter. It is not an abstract logo — it is the system, drawn in seven circles.

§ 01.A  ·  CONSTRUCTION

A 12 × 12 grid. Seven circles. No ornament.

The symbol lives inside a 12-unit square. The architect circle is 4 units in diameter; satellite workers are 2 units. Spokes run on 60° radials. Clearspace equals one full unit on every edge — never crowd the mark.

12u × 12u canvas 1u clearspace ⌀ 4u hub ⌀ 2u sat

Build specs

Canvas12 × 12 units, square. Marks render cleanly from 16 px (favicon) to architectural scale.
HubDiameter 4 units. Centered at (6, 6). Always terra cotta #da7756.
SatellitesDiameter 2 units. Placed on a circle of radius 6, at 0°/60°/120°/180°/240°/300°.
Spokes2.4 px stroke at 200-unit reference. Round caps. Same ink as satellites.
Hex perimeterConnects all 6 satellites. 55% opacity of ink — a whisper, not a frame.
Clearspace1 unit on every side. No type, rule or graphic may enter this zone.
Min size16 px (digital) · 8 mm (print). Below 24 px, swap to the simplified symbol (1.8).
