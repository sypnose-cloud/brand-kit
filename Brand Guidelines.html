BRAND PHILOSOPHY§ 02 · IRON RULE

▌ 02 · WHY SYPNOSE LOOKS LIKE SYPNOSE

The brand is the product.
The product has one rule.

"No proof.
No done.”

Every line of copy, every metric, every screenshot in this system must obey the same rule the orchestrator enforces on its workers: a claim is not a claim until a measurement supports it. If a sentence says "fast," it shows the number of milliseconds. If a sentence says "reliable," it shows the error count. If we don't have the number, we cut the sentence.

This is what gives the brand its voice — technical, terse, unornamented. We are not building trust through aesthetics; we are building trust through receipts. The aesthetics exist because receipts deserve a beautiful container.

Carlos De La Torre
Architect of SYPNOSE · Puerto Plata, DR · January 2026

SYPNOSE.CLOUDP. 02
LOGO · ANATOMY§ 03 · THE MARK

▌ 03 · ANATOMY OF THE MARK

Seven circles. Six spokes. One rule.

The Sypnose symbol is a literal diagram: one architect hub at the center, six worker satellites on a hex perimeter, six radial spokes for the A2A bus. It is not abstract — it is the system, drawn small.

▌ HUB · ARCHITECT · 4U Ø ▌ SATELLITE · WORKER · 2U Ø ▌ SPOKE · A2A BUS · 60° RADIAL ▌ HEX · PERIMETER · 55% OPACITY

Hub. Always terra cotta #DA7756. Never recolored. Diameter = 4 grid units.
Satellites. Slate or cream depending on ground. Diameter = 2 grid units.
Spokes. Round caps, 2.4 px stroke at the 200-unit reference frame.
Hex perimeter. Connects all six satellites at 55% opacity — a whisper, not a frame.

CONSTRUCTION GRID§ 05 · 12 × 12

▌ 05 · CONSTRUCTION GRID

12 × 12. Six radials.

Every approved variant sits inside a 12-unit square. The hub is 4u Ø, centered at (6,6). Satellites are 2u Ø, placed on a circle of radius 6 at 60° intervals.

12U × 12U CANVAS 1U CLEARSPACE ⌀ 4U HUB ⌀ 2U SAT

Reference grid 200u → 12u. Internal SVG works at 200-unit precision (16.66 × 16.66 per cell). Below 24 px, switch to the simplified silhouette (variant 1.8) — spokes and hex perimeter become visual noise.

SYPNOSE.CLOUDP. 05
CLEARSPACE§ 06 · 1U MARGIN

▌ 06 · CLEARSPACE

One unit. Always.

The clearspace zone is one grid unit on every side. No type, photo, illustration, ruling line or other graphic may enter this zone — including the wordmark itself when used as part of a lockup.

▌ MARGIN = 1U EQUAL ON ALL SIDES

In digital UI, 1u ≈ 8 px at the 96-px symbol size. In print, 1u ≈ 2 mm at a 24-mm symbol. Round up — never down.

SYPNOSE.CLOUDP. 06
MINIMUM SIZES§ 07 · 16PX FLOOR

▌ 07 · MINIMUM SIZES

16 px digital. 8 mm print.

Below those floors, the spokes and hex perimeter become illegible artifacts. The simplified silhouette (1.8) takes over — a terra hub on cream with six solid satellites, no spokes, no perimeter.

Print floor 8 mm. Below 8 mm in print, signal collapses on uncoated stock. For embroidery, foil or engraving, jump to 12 mm minimum and use variant 1.4 or 1.3.

PALETTE · PRIMARY§ 09 · THREE COLORS

▌ 09 · PRIMARY PALETTE

Three colors. No fourth.

The full Sypnose palette is exactly three colors. Terra cotta carries every accent. Cream is the daylight ground. Dark slate is the night ground. There is no secondary brand color — only functional tints (next page) for state UI.

VOICE & TONE§ 17 · IRON RULE

▌ 17 · VOICE & TONE

Terse. Measured. Receipts always.

Every line of copy obeys the Iron Rule. If you cannot put a number against a claim, you cannot make the claim. Short sentences. Specific verbs. Zero superlatives that don't carry a metric.

▌ DO · WITH RECEIPT

"300 workers in parallel. 741 ms A2A latency. 93% of PRs pass review on first attempt."

▌ DON'T · MARKETING SLOP

"Lightning-fast AI agents that unlock unprecedented productivity for cutting-edge teams."

▌ DO · DIRECT

"Five products in production. 314 paying clients. One VPS."

▌ DON'T · VAGUE

"Trusted by leading companies worldwide to power their AI transformation."

▌ DO · OWN THE RULE

"No proof, no done. Enforced in the orchestrator. Impossible to bypass."

▌ DON'T · OVERPROMISE

"Revolutionary. Best-in-class. The future of software engineering is here."
