▌ Block 03 — Sign-off System

Three styles.
Two themes.
Six signatures.

Every email Carlos sends is a credibility surface. Minimal for replies, Complete for new threads, Promotional when a 6-hour POC offer is live — each rendered for light and dark mail clients. Table-based HTML, web-safe stacks, terra cotta accent rule.

§ 03.A  ·  STYLE 01 · MINIMAL

For replies. Three lines and a wordmark.

When the thread is already established. Name, role, the domain. No links, no CTA, no badge — the Iron Rule applies to sign-offs too.
